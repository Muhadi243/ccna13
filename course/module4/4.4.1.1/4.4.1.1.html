Frame Relay Budget Proposal

It has been decided that your company will use Frame Relay technology to provide video connectivity between your main office location and two branch offices. The company will also use the new network for redundancy in case their current ISP network connectivity is interrupted for any reason.

As usual, with any kind of network upgrade, you must develop a cost proposal for your administrator.

After doing some research, you decide to use this Frame Relay web site for your cost analysis. Costs listed on the site are representative of real ISP costs – they are referenced only to help you create your cost analysis design.
